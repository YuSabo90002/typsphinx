<objective>

Prove it with a new GATE-01-pattern real-compile fixture that asserts the ABSENCE of the audit's exact cross-column collision signature, and update the one existing test whose byte-exact `columns:` assertion the fix invalidates.

Purpose: Move wide-table output from "collides + clips off the right margin" to "wraps within the text block, matching the HTML fidelity authority" â€” the milestone's central fidelity fix.
