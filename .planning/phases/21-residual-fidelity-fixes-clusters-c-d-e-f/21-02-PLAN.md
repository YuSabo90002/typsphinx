<objective>
Fix the two remaining "text-content emission" residual fidelity findings, each isolated to its own
`typsphinx/translator.py` handler, with a real-`typst.compile()` GATE-01 fixture per finding:

- **FID-11 (Cluster D — paragraph reflow):** reST soft/semantic line breaks inside a paragraph render as
  HARD line breaks (ragged short lines) instead of collapsing to a space. Root cause (RESEARCH.md D-Disc-1,
  verified): docutils merges a soft-wrapped paragraph into ONE `Text` node carrying a literal `\n`, and
  `escape_typst_string` turns it into a `\\n` escape that Typst decodes back to a forced break. Fix:
  collapse intra-paragraph `\n`→single space in `visit_Text` BEFORE escaping. Verification is structural
  `.typ` only — pypdf is NOT used (D-08, non-extractable vertical property; Phase-19 D-06 precedent).

- **FID-14 (Cluster F — abbr separator, narrow scope):** the auto-generated PEP 3102 `*` and PEP 570 `/`
  signature separators inject their `<abbr>` hover-title text inline ("* (Keyword-only parameters
  separator …)"). Root cause (RESEARCH.md D-Disc-3, verified via direct doctree inspection): the separator
  is an `abbreviation` node whose own `astext()` is exactly `"*"` or `"/"` — the sole reliable narrow
  signal. Fix: in `depart_abbreviation`, suppress the appended explanation ONLY when `node.astext()` is
  `"*"`/`"/"`; a genuine `:abbr:` KEEPS its inline expansion (D-Disc-3). Verification is Shape-B (structural
  + pypdf, D-10).

Purpose: continuation of the coherent v0.6.2 translator-fix series (independent, small-blast-radius edits).
Output: two `translator.py` edits + two new render-gate fixtures + one Shape-A (FID-11) and one Shape-B
(FID-14) gate module. Milestone invariant honored throughout.
</objective>
